About

This is the website for our prototype of a smart fridge. An old "dumb" fridge has been retrofitted with a Raspberry Pi and 3 wide-lens cameras.

The cameras allow us to see the latest contents of the fridge from 3 different angles.

We plan to add temperature sensors as well. Yes, multiple sensors! So that we can compare the temperature between the shelves.

Find out more about our other projects at monohelixlabs.com.
